How to escape

  
You're trapped in a weird house where everything moves — including the walls. If three doorways line up, even for a moment, you can dash for freedom.

  
The console controls the doorway nearest you (X/C) and the one furthest from you (N/M). The centre doorway has a mind of its own.

  
Footsteps are pounding down the corridor behind you. If they catch up before you escape… agghhhh!
