Play a retro text-based escape game inspired by 1980s Usborne computer books, where you control doorways in a shifting house to align three exits before footsteps catch you. Use keyboard controls (X/C and N/M) or on-screen buttons to move the near and far doorways left and right, while the center doorway moves unpredictably. The game features authentic CRT phosphor aesthetics with scanlines and a pulsing HUD that tracks your remaining time and door alignment status.
